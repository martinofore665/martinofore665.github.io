🎧 SonyBridge - Control Sony Headphones From Your Computer

Download SonyBridge for Windows

SonyBridge lets you change settings on your Sony headphones directly from your computer. You no longer need your phone to toggle noise cancelling, adjust the equalizer, or check your battery level. This application works on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

💡 Key Features

  • Noise Cancelling: Switch between noise cancelling, ambient sound mode, or turn features off.
  • Equalizer: Select preset sound profiles or create custom levels to match your music.
  • Battery Status: View the exact charge level of your headphones in real-time.
  • DSEE Settings: Toggle digital sound enhancement features to improve audio quality.
  • No Phone Needed: Connect your device via Bluetooth and start managing your settings immediately.

📥 Getting Started

Follow these steps to install and use SonyBridge on your Windows computer.

  1. Visit the Download Page: Go to the official releases page.
  2. Select Installer: Look for the latest file ending in .exe for Windows. Click the file to download it to your computer.
  3. Run the File: Locate the file in your Downloads folder and double-click it.
  4. Install: Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the setup process.
  5. Connect Headphones: Ensure your Sony headphones are paired with your computer via Bluetooth.
  6. Open Application: Launch SonyBridge from your Start menu to begin adjusting your headphone settings.

💻 System Requirements

SonyBridge runs on most modern Windows computers. Ensure your system meets these basic criteria:

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11.
  • Bluetooth radio enabled on your computer.
  • Compatible Sony headphones like the WH-1000XM4 or WH-CH720N series.

🛠 Troubleshooting

If you have trouble connecting, check the following items:

  • Make sure your headphones are not connected to another device, such as your phone, as this can block the computer from taking control.
  • Verify your computer's Bluetooth settings show the headphones as "Connected".
  • Restart the application if settings do not update instantly.
  • Ensure you have the latest drivers for your computer's Bluetooth adapter.
